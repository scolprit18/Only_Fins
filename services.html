What is soft washing?

Soft washing is the process of using chemicals to do the work of cleaning, instead of pressure. Your roof is extremely expensive, and it requires care. If properly cared for, it can last a long time. If not cared for, it can deteriorate, leak and just down right just look unappealing. It can also cause excess heat to be stored in the attic, which raises your electric bill. If you try to get the roof cleaned, but it's done improperly, you can do more damage than good. Using high pressure will destroy your shingles or tiles, and it can push water into your attic. This is detrimental to your roof, as well as a roof's warranty. It can also be immediately spotted by an insurance claim agent, and they will not pay to have your roof replaced. It needs to be done right. All roof's in Florida need to be cleaned every handful of years. The only way to do this the right way, is through a soft wash.

How do we seal pavers?

Pavers and Travertine need to be sealed every few years. The Florida sun is harsh, and we're not the only ones who feel it. The heat bakes the stones and slowly deteriorates the sealer. If your pavers are around a pool, the chemicals in pool water, or added foot traffic speed up the process as well. You can often see the difference between the locations that have shade and light foot traffic versus the areas in the direct sun. Once the sealer breaks down, it allows weeds to begin to grow through the joints in each stone. A proper sealant will bring this to a halt, and make everything look like new! Sealing the pavers requires the location to be emptied of all furniture and nik naks, so we can properly clean the location that will be sealed. Then, we will spend a few hours slowly getting paver sand into each joint, which helps keep each stone in place, where it belongs. Once the sand is filling each joint, we will apply the necessary amount of sealant, which is a 2 part water based epoxy. This is some heavy duty stuff, and gives a beautiful finished product.
